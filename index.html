how it works

Using advanced techniques like meristem isolation, we can eliminate viruses from the plant tissue, this ensures a clean healthy stock is returned.

We are staffed by a Ph.D. in genetics with extensive experience in tissue culture. Offering specialized and custom micropropagation for your elite plants.
Micropropagation facilitates the growth, storage, and maintenance of a large number of plants in small spaces which makes it a cost-effective process.

In our plant tissue culture lab, we can test for and remediate any pathogens and preserve the genetics.

We can then take that clean plant tissue and multiply it to many identical plants to meet your needs.

This gives you consistent stock that is healthy and vigorous!
Diagnostics

Plant Pathology 

Offering RT qPCR Assays 

  

  The gold standard of detecting pathogens, we use the most advanced technology allowing for fast acurate results even at low pathogen levels, giving you the anwsers you need before the infections can spread through-out.   

 

 Using this technique we can identify a wide array of diseases caused by fungi, viruses, and phytoplasma.                 

Custom High throughput sequencing (HTS)

HTS is very rapid efficient way to sequence unknown samples DNA/RNA. This provides a huge advange to identify a possible infection without prior knowlege of the pathogen. 

 

   If we have exhausted all other possible methods HTS can be used to isolate out the possible causes of disease in the plant sample.  Then trained staff will establish the biological significance of each sequence and supply you with a comprehensive anylysis and possible treatment plans.
